Ingredients:

  • 6 flour tortilla wraps
  • Beef mincemeat
  • Nacho chips
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • Nacho cheese sauce

How to make:

  1. Brown the beef mincemeat and add the taco seasoning according to package instructions.
  2. While you are cooking the mincemeat, start heating up your nacho cheese sauce in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Warm your tortilla wraps in a 90ÂºC oven for about 5 minutes.
  4. Lay out a tortilla wrap on a plate.
  5. Add broken up nacho chips to the middle of the tortilla wrap.
  6. Pour nacho cheese sauce over the nacho chips.
  7. Top with cooked mincemeat.
  8. Add more nacho cheese on top.
  9. Fold or roll the Naco up and enjoy!
